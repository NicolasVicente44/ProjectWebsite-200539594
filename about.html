About Us

A family run small business in simcoe county for 20 years

We've been located right here on the beach serving simcoe county with the highest quality automotive detailing in the city. By professionals, for professionals.


Hand wash, wax, and tire-shine

Exceptional hand wash and waxes using our own superior product line, we can remove years of salt, mud, and whatever else has ruined your ride and its wheels.

Full interior package

Years of dust, stains, fading, dirt, and damage can be reversed in just one visit. Nothing can add to both the value and comfort of your vehicle then a restored and clean interior. We even offer vinyl and seat protective packages.

2 Stage paint correction

We offer professional 2 stage paint correction. Starting with a compound buffing we bring paint damage to the surface, then use polish to remove them with high powered buffers and fresh pads.

Clay barring

We can remove surface fallout damage, sap and particles stuck in your paint, returning it back to factory like smoothness and shine. When combined with our paint correction package, your ride will be returned to it's exquisite shine and health

We offer premium products too

Premium high quality low priced detailing supplies for individuals and small businesses alike. Paint polish, waxes, body-shines, tire shines, degreaser, and more.
